I live in Hilversum, the Netherlands — about 30 minutes east of Amsterdam, where I was born and raised.
At home I'm joined by my wife [Jolie](https://jolie.nl/) and our daughter Rosalie,
while our son Lexander has since flown the nest and now lives in Utrecht.

After 24 years of freelancing, I've recently joined [Speer IT](https://speerit.nl/),
where I work on fiber optics registration and asset management software.

## Projects

### Game of Life

Back in 1996, I built a Java applet just to teach myself the language and add some interactivity to the web.
It's been rewritten a couple of times since, but I had no idea it would turn into my most visited website
and still running all these years later.

- [playgameoflife.com](https://playgameoflife.com/)
- [Game of Life source](https://github.com/edwinm/game-of-life)

### Webontwikkelaar

A daily digest of conferences, articles, videos and podcasts for web developers.
Originally built for the Dutch-speaking community, but fully available in English too.

- [webontwikkelaar.nl](https://webontwikkelaar.nl/en)
- [webontwikkelaar.nl source](https://github.com/edwinm/webontwikkelaar.nl)
